Clair Cameron Patterson (1922-1995)

The geochemist who saved us from lead poisoning


A snapshot of Clair Cameron Patterson's most significant achievements.

  • In 1956 C.C. Paterson calculated the age of the Earth - 4.55 billion years, a value which remained largely unchanged to this day. Before Patterson's work the age of the Earth was estimated to be approximately 3.3 billion years.

  • C.C. Paterson's second important discovery was that humans were responsible for the lead contamination of the environment on the massive scale. The agent responsible for that was mainly a gasoline additive - tetraethyllead.

  • C.C. Paterson dedicated the rest of his carrer for advocating the reduction of environmental lead contamination based on scientfic evidence. In was mainly his relentless efforts that eventually lead to the worldwide ban of leaded gasoline.
